Admission to B.Tech programs typically requires completion of 10+2 (with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) and a valid score in JEE Main, AP EAPCET, or the MBU Entrance Test (MBUCET). MBU also considers merit in these exams for selection.

Yes. Scholarships are available based on merit, entrance exam performance, and economic need. Programs like MBUCET may carry scholarship provisions for high-ranking applicants.
