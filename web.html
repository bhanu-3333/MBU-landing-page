About Us

Empowering Students,
Inspiring Futures

At Mohan Babu University (MBU), education is more than academics—it’s about nurturing leaders, innovators, and changemakers. With a proud legacy of 33+ years, MBU has transformed into a hub of world-class learning, research, and industry collaboration.

We believe in providing students with not just knowledge, but also the right skills, values, and exposure to thrive in a global environment. From cutting-edge programs in Engineering, Business, Computer Applications, Agriculture, and Sciences to our prestigious Joint Degree with Penn State University, MBU stands as a gateway to opportunities worldwide.
